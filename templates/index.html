What country best suites you? As young college students, the world is the pearl in our hands. We set out to find the best country that suites the factors that we want you to control. Using inputs from you, data from these countries, and a bit of machine learning magic, we predict the best country for you, as well as the chance that other countries would fit you. Our dataset is a combination of csv files that we found on Kaggle, and they included information on pollution, happiness, and population, which are important factors when considering a country to live in. In terms of machine learning, we are using a Support Vector Machine classifier. In order to feed in the data, we scaled it with a Robust Scaler in order to remove the impact of outliers (particularily the United States and India, which as metrics far exceeding other countries). In the end, we used the same model to predict the probability of all other countries (in our dataset) that could match you.
